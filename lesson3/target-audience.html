Target Audience

The target audience is the general community of sneads ferry, tourists, and military personnel.

Personas

Persona 1: This is someone who lives here and is looking to get into the community. They are a man or woman who has a family and needs resources to get involved in the community and find local businesses.

Persona 2: This is a Marine who just got stationed here at Camp Lejeune. They are in their early twenties and is looking for places to hang out or eat. They might also be looking into programs for community service.

Scenarios

Whats the weather going to be for the beach today?

What are local shopping and restaurants?

What are some local events being held this month?

Where can I go shopping for groceries?

What do I do if theres a hurricane?
