North Topsail beach is only an 8 minute drive from Sneads Ferry. This is the main attraction for tourists during the summer months.

The boat dock is the main area for launching boats in Sneads Ferry. This area gives access to the Intercoastal Waterway and can take you all the way down to Wilmington or even further.

The "Shark Store" is a term used by the locals, for obvious reasons. This is the #1 souvenir store in Sneads Ferry.

The Shrimp Festival occurs every September and this year will be on Sept. 8-9.

The Golf Course is the only one in the area. This beautiful and well run course is ran year round.
